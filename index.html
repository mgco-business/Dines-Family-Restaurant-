Our Story

A family table, open to all of Durham

Dine's Family Restaurant & Deli has been Oshawa's go-to for honest, home-cooked food longer than most regulars can remember. Friendly faces, generous plates, and a pot of coffee that never runs dry.

We do it a little differently: we grill instead of deep-fry — yes, even the home fries — so even the most health-conscious guest leaves happy and full. That's the Dine's promise.
